I'm a Marketing & Communications specialist with a degree in Advertising from Universidad de Palermo, Argentina. My experience spans brand launches, digital campaigns, e-commerce management, content strategy, and performance marketing — across consumer goods, wine & spirits, and real estate.

What sets me apart is my ability to operate across the full marketing funnel: from defining brand identity and communication pillars, to running Meta Ads campaigns that deliver measurable ROAS, to managing every digital touchpoint that connects a brand to its audience.

I'm equally comfortable leading a zero-to-one brand launch as I am optimizing a complex e-commerce operation or orchestrating a multi-market influencer campaign. I believe great marketing lives at the intersection of creative vision and hard data.
